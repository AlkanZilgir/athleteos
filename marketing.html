Questions

If you've got more, just ask the AI coach.

Is it really free?

The free tier never expires and doesn't need a credit card. You get workouts, cardio, nutrition tracking, sleep, body metrics, and up to 7 AI coach messages a day + 10 exercise demos a day. Pro removes those limits — unlimited AI, unlimited templates, unlimited exercise demos, and more progress photos — for a small monthly or yearly subscription.

Where does my data live?

In your own row in a managed Postgres database (Supabase). Encrypted in transit and at rest. You can export everything as JSON from Settings any time.

Does it work on iPhone?

Yes — open the site in Safari, tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen." Once installed it runs full-screen, works offline, and supports notifications on iOS 16.4+.

Why a web app and not a native one?

One codebase, instant updates, no App Store gatekeeper. Trade-off: no Apple Watch yet. We're working on it.

Can I cancel and take my data with me?

Settings → Export my data dumps everything as JSON. Settings → Delete account erases it for good.
