Import health data

Bring weights, cardio sessions and sleep into AthleteOS from your phone. We dedupe by date so re-imports are safe.

How to get my file
Apple Health (iPhone)
  1. Open Health app → tap your profile picture (top-right).
  2. Scroll to Export All Health Data → wait for the zip.
  3. Share the zip to your phone's files, unzip, and upload the file named export.xml here.
Google Fit (Android)
  1. Go to takeout.google.com, deselect all, then pick only Fit.
  2. Export and download the zip.
  3. Unzip and upload the Daily activity metrics.csv or All Sessions.csv file here.
